Welcome to ErbslandFORMER documentation!

ErbslandFORMER is a web-based tool for mass text transformations, ideal for writers, translators, software documentation authors, academic paper writers, and developers. It supports large language models and regular expressions, offering a streamlined workflow for importing, transforming, reviewing, and exporting documents.
